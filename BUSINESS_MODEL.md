# Shape The Wave Longevity™ — Business Model

**Optimize health. Align habits. Live longer, better.**

> One document covering the full commercial offering: **5 memberships, 16 bundles, 21 packages, and 49 services**, aligned to the locked brand architecture — Shape The Wave Longevity™ → REEL™ Align Method™ → ReelVerse AI™ → ReelVerse OS™. _Prices are illustrative starting points; adjust to your market._

## How the offerings fit together (the offer ladder)

- **Services (49)** — individual, à-la-carte offerings. The atomic units of value.
- **Packages (21)** — curated sets of services aimed at one specific outcome.
- **Bundles (16)** — multi-package, cross-pillar transformations, often paired with a membership term.
- **Memberships (5)** — recurring access to ReelVerse OS and the REEL Align Method.

Everything is organized around the five movements of the **REEL Align Method™**: **Reflect · Envision · Execute · Learn · Align.**
